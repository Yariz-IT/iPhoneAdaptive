What's news

All models are equipped with a single-chip A16 Bionic system, 48-megapixel main camera and ProMotion screens with Ceramic Shield protection and a frequency range of 1 - 120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The iPhone 14 Pro came out in a new design - without the branded “bangs”. Also in the range appeared new color. The smartphone, unlike the "regular" 14 iPhones, has an A16 processor Bionic, which can perform 4 trillion photo operations per second.
